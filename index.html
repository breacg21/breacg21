Visual Dream

The Idea:
I call this "device" Visual Dream. It's basically a pillow, but it's different from others because inside the pillow for a few reasons. Inside the pillow, there would be sensors all over, and those sensors are picking up your brain waves using the different parts of your brain. The waves would then be calculated, measured, and stored to create multiple pictures that would play the same way a typical video would.

Save-A-Thought

The Idea:
I don't know about you, but I forget important things all the time. Quite frequently I'll keep thinking of something, but no matter what I do, I forget about it as soon as it becomes relevant. So, what if there was a small device that would stick to you and it could record everything you think about, and put it into writing or a recording for you? It would be small and stick right behind your ear. It could link to your phone or device of choice through bluetooth, and all of your thoughts could be at the tip of your fingers in both voice recordings and/or regular notes.
